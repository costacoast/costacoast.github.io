HELLO WORLD!

I am a creative technologist interested in human cognition and how we interact with technology. I spend most of my time making things for the web, tinkering with new technologies, and figuring out how they best fit into the human condition. When I'm not building digital things, I like to make stuff with my hands, work on cars, and explore places I've never been.

With over nine years of digital and strategic consulting experience I have learned to manage budgets, clients, people, and most importantly expectations with ease. My passion is in designing user interfaces that people can naturally interact with, and creating simple elegant solutions to complex problems.
