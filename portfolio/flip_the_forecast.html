Nearing the end of a long, cold, winter we gave a group of lucky Bostonians a chance to warm up on the sunny shores of San Diego. The Catch? They had to convince a San Diegan to come take their place.

JetBlue Card is the airline card that is known for helping you “Fly Faster Than You Think”, and true to their motto we got people out of the cold at the drop of a hat. Before they could stamp their complimentary ticket, they first needed to convince a local from San Diego to take their place. 

We built a proprietary live-streaming booth that could stream while recording 4k footage from three cameras inside each of our fully customized airstream trailers. Then we took our show on the road for some surprise and delight, setting one in snowy downtown Boston and the other one in sunny San Diego.

The one day experiential film shoot drew people from all walks of life and asked them to offer up their best sales pitch. Some begged for mercy, others bragged about the virtues of not being able to feel your own toes, and one person even tried to claim that Boston had better Mexican food…than San Diego. At the end of the day we learned Bostonians would say just about anything for a break from the cold, and San Diegans were shockingly accommodating.
