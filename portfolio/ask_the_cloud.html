As it turns out, even CTO’s have a lot of questions about the cloud. So we decided to give Google Cloud a voice and let them get answers straight from the source.

Tasked with the goal of increasing brand awareness and sales conversion for the worlds largest tech company is never a small task. Learning that Google was in a distant third place behind Amazon & Microsoft in a market driven primarily by fear and price made that task all the more challenging.

Facing the impossible, we found a way to flip these challenges into enormous strengths. A bit of research allowed us to learn that most CTO’s didn’t even understand what to do with the industry-leading technology we were trying to sell. And it wasn’t just Google’s cloud services they were unsure of—they simply had a lot of questions and no one to ask.

So we built a Google Cloud powered voice assistant with the smarts to answer questions about, well, itself.

Then we reached out to our top prospects in the United States and United Kingdom, inviting them to ask the cloud through one of the 5,000+ devices that support our Google Assistant.

  • The results were outstanding:

  • - 1,000+ sessions per month on average

  • - 8+ minutes per session on average

  • - 17% direct mail response rate

  • - Increased both sales meetings and conversions
